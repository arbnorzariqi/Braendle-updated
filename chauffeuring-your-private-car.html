Chauffeuring your private car

Enjoy the luxuries you’re used to even while you’re away from home. From the absence of your driver to an out of town celebration or work trip, there are many reasons you might find yourself away from home with your own car and nobody to drive you within your comfort.

No problem. Contact our team and we will provide you with a highly experienced, expertly trained, certified Braendle Chauffeur. Your personal chauffeur will be at your disposal at any time, available to drive you safely, comfortably and discretely to meetings, appointments and events as wished.

We would also be delighted to provide you with a chauffeur for longer periods of time, for example in the event of holiday or illness of your regular driver. Please call +41 76 517 77 54 or email info@braendle-limousine.ch to find out more.
